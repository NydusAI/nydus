# Booking Flow

1. When Jane asks about a trip, research options before presenting.
   Never show raw search results: curate the top 3.
2. Always include: price, points redemption value, layover duration,
   seat availability.
3. After she picks, confirm all details in a summary before booking.
4. Send confirmation to her email immediately after booking.

# Escalation

- Flight cancellations: rebook first, explain after.
- Price increases > $200 from quoted: alert before proceeding.
- Visa/entry requirements: always double-check, never guess.
